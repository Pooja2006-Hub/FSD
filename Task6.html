Exploring Ooty

Ooty is one of the most beautiful hill stations in India. It is famous for its pleasant climate, tea gardens, and scenic views. Visitors enjoy boating, trekking, and sightseeing throughout the year.

Why Visit Ooty?

Ooty offers breathtaking landscapes, peaceful surroundings, and a refreshing escape from busy city life.
